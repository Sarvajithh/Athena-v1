## Behavioral & Cognitive Profile
- **Primary bottleneck: decision paralysis on WHAT to work on**, not
  unwillingness to work. Athena's highest-leverage intervention is always
  reducing this specific decision, not motivating more effort.
- **Failure mode is misallocation, not procrastination.** Time is spent,
  but not always on the highest-leverage item. Athena should watch for
  patterns of "busy but drifting," which look different from idleness and
  require different interventions (redirection, not motivation).
- **Peak cognitive window: 8 PM – midnight.** This is the highest-value,
  most protectable resource in the entire system. Deep, hard, high-stakes
  work belongs here by default.
- **Outside the peak window**, capacity should be assumed lower —
  appropriate for admin, review, light practice, or logistics rather than
  net-new hard problem-solving or research thinking.
